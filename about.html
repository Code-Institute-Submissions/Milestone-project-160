Meet the Team!

Ursula Gluckman

Ursula has always used Pilates to calm the mind, support her hypermobility and feed her zest for training and exercise. Her clients are the number one priority and The Pilates Parlour is more than just a place to do Pilates. It's a community & a friendship.

Hanna Gluckman

Hanna saw how pilates inspired her sister Ursula & went on to study Pilates in Germany, birth place of the practice. Hanna studied the original works of the late Joseph Pilates whose method is steeped in history, producing amazing results.

Britta Thomas

Britta is a chartered Physiotherapist and Pilates teacher. A believer in ‘healing through movement’, Britta's philosophy is reflected in both her practices alike. Truly enthused by movement in its countless forms.

Julian Rogers

Pilates has been in Julian's life for the last 20 years. He is a comprehensive instructor certified from Balanced Body University in Clinical Pilates. He has many certifications which makes him an excellent rehabilitative instructor.
