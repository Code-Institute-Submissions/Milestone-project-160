About The Pilates Parlour

Get Ready to feel longer, leaner and stronger!

Ursula Gluckman

Suffering from her own physical restraints, Ursula had always used Pilates to calm the mind, support her hypermobility and to feed her zest for training & exercise. Her clients are the number one priority and The Pilates Parlour is more than just a place to do Pilates. It is a community & a friendship.

Hanna Gluckman

Younger sister to Ursula, Hanna saw how pilates helped and inspired her sister & went on to study Pilates in Mönchengladbach, Germany, home town of founder Joseph Pilates. Hanna has studied the original works of the late Joseph Pilates whose method is proven and steeped in history, producing amazing results.

Britta Thomas

Britta is a chartered Physiotherapist and Pilates teacher. Steadfast believer in ‘healing through movement’, Britta's philosophy is reflected in her physiotherapy and Pilates practices alike. Cares less about perfection and far more about connection. Truly enthused by movement in its countless forms.

Julian Rogers

Julian had a passion for sports and movement. Pilates has been in his life for the last 10 years, 8 of those as a professional trainer. He is a comprehensive instructor certified from Balanced Body University and particularly in Clinical Pilates. He has many certifications which makes him an excellent rehabilitative instructor.
