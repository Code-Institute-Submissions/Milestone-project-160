Welcome to the Pilates Parlour

We're thrilled you stopped by and can't wait to meet you in our awesome studio!

Ursula Gluckman

Ursula lives and breathes Pilates. It supports her mind, hypermobility and feeds her zest for training & exercise. Her clients are the number one priority and The Pilates Parlour is more than just a place to do Pilates. It's a community & a friendship.

Hanna Gluckman

Younger sister to Ursula, Hanna saw how pilates inspired her sister & went on to study Pilates in Germany, birth place of founder Joseph Pilates. Hanna studied the original works of the late Joseph Pilates whose method is steeped in history, producing amazing results.

Britta Thomas

Britta is a chartered Physiotherapist and Pilates teacher. Britta is truly enthused by movement in its countless forms. Her continuous learning and cutting edge techniques makes her invaluable to The Pilates Parlour. Her clients adore her insights.

Julian Rogers

Julian had a passion for sports and movement. For the last 10 years he has been a professional Pilates trainer. He is a comprehensive instructor certified from Balanced Body Uni in Clinical Pilates. His many certifications which makes him an excellent rehabilitative instructor.
