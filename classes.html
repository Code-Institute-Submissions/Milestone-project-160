Mat Pilates

Our most popular classes are our mat Pilates classes. Taught across 4 levels, from beginners to intermediate we have a class to suit everyone’s ability. We teach mainly STOTT Pilates with modifications, regressions & progressions depending on our clients ability.

Barre Pilates

Our Ballet Barre class is a fun cardio workout designed using traditional ballet moves and utilising the Pilates basic principles. Our 50/50 Barre & Pilates course combines 25 minutes cardio Barre with 25 minutes of Pilates mat based core work.

Prenatal

Prenatal Pilates is suitable for pregnant women from 12 weeks right up to your due date. Our exercise program starts with exercises on the birthing ball and moves to mat exercises. We also use small equipment such as flexi bands & mini balls. It’s a good toning class which will keep you fit, strong & pain free throughout your pregnancy.

TRX

TRX is a suspension, body weight training system. It's a great full body resistance workout! Just by adjusting the angle of your body, your stance and position, you alter the level of intensity. It progresses with you.
